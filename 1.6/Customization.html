There are many ways to customize your MyBB.

Templates and Themes allow you to customize how your forum looks.

Plugins and Modifications allow you to add additional functionality to your forum. Plugins are the easiest to use and typically do not require any file modification. Modifications require you to manually edit the MyBB files.

Translations allow you to translate the MyBB interface into a different language.
